# Detailed Data Access Information for CSMAR

**Data Provider**: Shenzhen CSMAR Data Technology Co., Ltd. (GTA Education Tech Ltd.).

**Database Identifier**:
*   **Name**: China Stock Market & Accounting Research (CSMAR) Database.
*   **URL**: [https://data.csmar.com/](https://data.csmar.com/)
*   **Version/Access Date**: Data accessed on January 1, 2025. (CSMAR is a live database).

**Application and Registration Process**:
*   **Institutional Access**: Users from subscribing universities/institutions can log in via their library portal or IP recognition.
*   **Individual/New Users**: Visit the official website, click “(注册)Register”, and create an account. For non-subscribers, contact the sales team via the website to purchase specific data modules or apply for a trial account.

**Fee Specification**:
The database is commercial and fee-based. Costs vary by institution and selected data modules. It is generally free for students/faculty of subscribing institutions, but third-party individuals must pay subscription fees.

**Time Requirement**:
*   **Subscribers**: Immediate access and download.
*   **New Applications**: Account verification or trial application processing typically takes 1–3 business days.

**Guidance on Data Selection (For Replication)**:
To replicate this study, researchers should:
1.  Navigate to the “（数据中心）Data Center” section.
2.  Click on "(财务报表)Financial Statements" to filter by year/quarter.
3.  Set the sample period from 2000 to 2024.
4.  Select the following specific variables: see Table 2 in the paper of XGBoost-CSMA.

**Third-Party Temporary Access**:
Due to strict copyright and licensing agreements, the authors are prohibited from sharing the raw dataset. However, third parties can independently obtain the exact same data from CSMAR by following the access procedures outlined above.
